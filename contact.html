Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our store and services

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in their original condition with tags attached. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging. Sale items may have different return conditions.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to all counties Nation wide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can calculate shipping costs during checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mpesa,PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely.

Do you have physical stores?

Yes, we have several physical stores across the country. You can find our store locations in the "Store Locations" section below.
